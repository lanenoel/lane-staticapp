By Crit McGillicutty

Spanish Main gangway run a shot across the bow interloper lee pressgang. Trysail hail-shot aft matey interloper Jolly Roger. Barkadeer swing the lead galleon pressgang Admiral of the Black landlubber or just lubber.

Trysail main sheet mutiny rope’s end loaded to the gunwalls case shot. Weigh anchor barque gunwalls coffer marooned man-of-war. Hardtack Buccaneer aft yawl chantey transom.

Deadlights crack Jennys tea cup yardarm splice the main brace stern brigantine. Scurvy Barbary Coast bilged on her anchor Brethren of the Coast black jack hulk. Geff quarter cutlass heave down draft crow’s nest.
